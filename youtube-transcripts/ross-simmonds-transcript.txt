## Key Insights
1. **Distribution > Creation**
   - Spent 20% on creating, 80% on distributing
   - Repurpose content across multiple channels
   - Reach audience where they are

2. **Content Should Move Buyers**
   - Don't just create awareness
   - Help buyers make decisions
   - Connect content to buying stages

3. **Visibility Is Everything**
   - A great post is worthless if no one sees it
   - Promote content multiple times
   - Use different formats for same content

## Why This Matters for B2B SaaS
Ross provides practical guidance on turning content into pipeline. His distribution-first approach is critical for SaaS companies competing for attention.
