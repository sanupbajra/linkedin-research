# Justin Welsh - Podcast & Interview Resources

## Who Is Justin Welsh?
- Solopreneur who built a $5M+ business
- Known for "Systematic Content Creation" framework
- Active daily on LinkedIn with high-engagement posts
- No YouTube channel - focuses on written content

## Key Podcast Appearances

### 1. My First Million Podcast
**Episode:** "How to Build a $5M Solopreneur Business"
**Key Takeaways:**
- Started with no audience, posted daily for 2+ years
- Focused on serving one specific audience
- Revenue came from trust built through consistent content

### 2. The SaaS Podcast
**Episode:** "LinkedIn Organic Growth Strategy"
**Key Takeaways:**
- Write for one person, not the masses
- Share real experiences, not theory
- Engage with comments to build community

## Justin's Content System

### Framework for Daily Posts
1. **Hook** - First 2 lines must stop the scroll
2. **Story** - Personal experience or lesson
3. **Value** - Actionable advice or framework
4. **Call to Action** - Ask a question or suggest next step

### Topics He Covers
- Solopreneur business building
- LinkedIn growth tactics
- Content creation systems
- Personal branding
- Email newsletter growth

## Why This Matters for B2B SaaS
Justin demonstrates that organic LinkedIn content can build a multi-million dollar business without paid advertising. His systematic approach is replicable for SaaS founders.
